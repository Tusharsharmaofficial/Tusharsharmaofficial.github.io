About

Hello this is Tushar Sharma – a singer, anchor, host, voice-over artist, and performer who lives and breathes music. For me, music is a way to connect, inspire, and share emotions.

Along with singing, I also shine as an anchor and presenter, bringing passion and energy to every stage. Off stage, I’m a medical student, blending compassion with creativity – a journey of medicine and melodies.

On YouTube and social media, I share songs, covers, and creative videos, vlogs, building a growing community of listeners. This is just the beginning of my musical journey!

If you love music with heart and energy, you’re in the right place. Let’s vibe together!
